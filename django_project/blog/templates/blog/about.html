Medhelp is a platform meant to deepen the connection betweeen a medic and his/her patients. This is achieved via a series of interactions between the two, which normally would occur face to face.

Our platform breaks down the difficulties behind these types of interactions by having a remote connection between the two parties. The website provides a series of functionalities regarding appointments and prescriptions, all of which have made the way one interacts with one's medic much simpler.
